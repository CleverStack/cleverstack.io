What is
Cleverstack?

In an effort to make rapid prototyping of web apps smarter, faster and easier, the team at CleverTech decided to produce a tool for easy frontend and backend development. CleverStack makes rapid prototyping of your next project easy through the use of the latest open source development tools used by companies like Google.

CleverStack is an ecosystem of tools built by the open source community supporting the Angular.JS and Node.JS projects, combined with pieces the CleverTech team has built to help make things faster and smarter for our clients.
